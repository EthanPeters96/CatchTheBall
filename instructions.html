How to Play

  • Click the start button to begin the game
  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move the basket
  • Catch balls to score points - every 5 points advances you to the next level
  • Reach level 10 to win the game
  • You have 10 lives - missing a ball costs one life
  • Game over will reset you to level 1
  • The game gets progressively harder as you level up
  • Good luck!
