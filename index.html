Instructions

  • Step 1: Click on the "Start Game" button to begin the game.
  • Step 2: Move the basket left or right using the arrow keys to catch the falling balls.
  • Each time you catch a ball, your score will increase. Once you catch 5 balls, you proceed to the next level.
  • Step 4: Be careful not to let any balls pass you, as it will increase your missed count. If the missed count reaches 10, your game will be end.
  • step 5: As you progress, the level will increase and the balls and basket will get faster.
  • Step 6: Once you reach level 10, you win the game!
  • Step 7: To stop the game, click on the "Stop Game" button.
  • Step 8: Have fun and enjoy the game!
